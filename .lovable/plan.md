

# Rachel D.K. Clark — Wearable Art Portfolio Website

A single-page, visually driven website showcasing Rachel's wearable art garments, workshops, fashion shows, and biography. Clean, bold, and artistic — letting the garments carry the visual weight.

---

## Navigation
- Fixed top navigation bar with anchor links: **Garments · Fashion Shows · Workshops · About · Book Rachel**
- Smooth scrolling between sections
- Clean logo/name text on the left: "Rachel D.K. Clark"
- Mobile-friendly hamburger menu

---

## 1. Hero Section
- Full-width hero with large placeholder image area (ready for garment photos)
- Bold headline: *"Wearable Art That Speaks."*
- Subheadline: *"Contemporary folk art, fashion shows, and workshops by Rachel D.K. Clark."*
- Brief intro paragraph about Rachel's 50+ years of experience
- Three call-to-action buttons: **View Garments · Book Rachel · Explore Workshops**
- Vibrant accent colors (turquoise, red, blue, yellow) used sparingly

## 2. Garments Gallery
- Intro text about Rachel's range of work (whimsical to political to personal)
- Filterable image gallery organized by collection:

- Large image cards with styled placeholders ready for photo uploads

- Clean grid layout that adapts from 3 columns (desktop) to 1 column (mobile)

## Visual Style
- **Background:** Clean white with subtle neutral sections for visual rhythm
- **Accent colors:** Turquoise, red, blue, yellow — used in buttons, borders, and highlights
- **Typography:** Modern sans-serif (clean and contemporary)
- **Layout:** Large image blocks, generous whitespace, minimal clutter
- **Responsive:** Fully mobile-friendly design
- All image areas built with styled placeholders ready for easy photo swaps
